20/30 Labs Ltd is a UKAS accredited testing laboratory (No. 4236) in accordance with ISO 17025, and was established in 2005 to perform microbiological analysis and research into hospital decontamination.
Over the years 20/30 Labs has gained vast industry knowledge and contacts and is today considered to be the UK’s leading microbiological consultancy specialising in the microbiological analysis of waters, the decontamination of medical devices (specifically endoscopes), automated washer-disinfectors and endoscope drying cabinets.
As the trusted technical resource for the principal equipment manufacturers, we set the gold standard for every aspect of water and decontamination equipment analysis from identifying the problem to developing innovative solutions for fault resolution. All samples are treated with the highest laboratory standard and adherence to regulations is controlled and maintained at all times.
With a passion for infection prevention and years of experience, 20/30 Labs continually adapts to the ever-changing environment. With a new, purpose-built 20,000 sq. ft. of laboratory space, including a unique research facility, and employing highly qualified and dedicated scientists, 20/30 Labs can offer the complete microbiological service. 20/30 Labs prides itself in providing customers with a reliable, versatile and personal service with an emphasis on quality assurance.
20/30 Labs Ltd have the expertise to provide clients with specialist research and development testing. Accredited to BS EN ISO 13485 for “The provision of microbiological testing and consultancy for the medical device industry” we are able to offer a wide range of microbiological validation services for medical devices including the determination of decontamination efficacy and the enumeration of microbiological growth in medical device storage systems.
